Modelling of AQI using Count Time Series

2024-08-13

Mean & variance of the data

From the given Time Series data, the calculated mean = 7.425926 and variance = 114.6435. So, we can consider it as a Overdispersed data.

Time Series and Barplot of the data

From the above plot a very strong seasonality and large number of zeroes are observed.

Proposed Model

A proposed model for the seasonal data may be -

\[ X_t = \alpha \odot X_{t-12} + \epsilon_t \qquad \alpha\in(0,1) \]

The marginal distribution of error term is given by - \[ P(\epsilon_t = i) \quad = \quad \left\{ \begin{array}{ll} \ \alpha+(1-\alpha)(1-\theta), & \ if \ i=0 \\ \ (1-\alpha)\theta^i(1-\theta), & \ if \ i=1,2,3,... \end{array} \right. \]

Conditional Probability Distribution

The conditional probability distribution of the above process is given by -

\[ P(X_t=x_t|X_{t-12}=x_{t-12})=\sum_{k=0}^{min(x_t,x_{t-12})}\binom{x_{t-12}}{k}\alpha^k(1-\alpha)^{x_{t-12}-k}\{(\alpha+(1-\alpha)(1-\theta))I_{x_t-k=0}+(1-\alpha)(1-\theta)\theta^{x_t-k}I_{x_t-k\neq 0}\} \]

Conditional Likelihood Function

The conditional log-likelihood function is given by -

\[ \ l_n(\theta,\alpha ) = \sum_{t=13}^{n} \log P(X_t=x_{t} \mid X_{t-12}=x_{t-12}) \]

Simulation Study

After maximizing the above log-likelihood function numerically I get \(\alpha =0.6177842\) and success probability of geometric distribution = 0.1084501.
